Wim Louw

Data Scientist · City of Cape Town, South Africa

I work in the City of Cape Town's Data Analytics Hub alongside data engineers, helping departments use their data to improve services, answer questions, and solve problems.

Before that, I was Research Manager at J-PAL Africa, where I spent several years running randomised controlled trials and field experiments in youth employment, education, and administrative data.

My interests span development economics, causal inference, natural language processing, and civic tech. I have an MSc in Social Research Methods from the London School of Economics and an MA in Linguistics from the University of KwaZulu-Natal.
